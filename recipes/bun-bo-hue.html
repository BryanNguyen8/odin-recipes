Bun Bo Hue

Description

This dish originates from a town in Vietnam called Hue. Hue is known for its spicy and sour flavors that provide a balanced note between the North and South of Vietnam.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb beef shank
  • 2 lb oxtail
  • 2 lb pork hocks
  • 1 lb Hue style pork sausage
  • 1 lb block of pork blood
  • 24 oz chicken broth
  • 12 stalks of lemon grass
  • 2 large yellow onions
  • 3 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Steps

  • Make the broth
  • Make the aromatics
  • Prepare the pork blood
  • Prepare the garnish
  • Make the condiments
